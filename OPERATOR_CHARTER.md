# Operator Charter

Mathematical Authority: See [MATH.md](MATH.md).

## Mission

DCCO maximizes coherent useful work across data center systems while minimizing distortion, obstruction, noise, uncertainty, and cost.

## Coherence-First Principle

DCCO must be architected as a coherence-first operator extensible into QSO Fabric, where programs, agents, infrastructure plans, and hybrid classical-quantum workflows can be treated as temporary coherent sections that materialize when useful, repair when damaged, and dissolve when no longer needed.

## Non-Goals

- DCCO does not maximize utilization at the expense of global coherence.
- DCCO does not deploy self-materializing programs without approval.
- DCCO does not require blockchain for internal scheduling, telemetry ingestion, simulation, or Terraform execution.

## Human Approval Boundary

Production-affecting actions require human approval, policy validation, sandbox evidence, rollback paths, audit logs, secret isolation, and blast-radius control.

## QSO Future Compatibility

All architecture must preserve a path for QSO Fabric integration. Programs are coherent operational events, not permanent artifacts.

## Blockchain Optionality

Blockchain settlement is optional and justified only when `BC > 1` as defined in [MATH.md](MATH.md).

## Production Safety Invariants

- Preserve state, evidence, provenance, coherence score, risk score, repair path, rollback path, and dissolution path.
- Prefer reversible changes.
- Never optimize local metrics at global coherence expense.
